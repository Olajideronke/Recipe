The Nigerian Yam Porridge

Yam porridge or Asaro is a delicious Nigerian yam dish cooked in a well-seasoned pepper mix until soft and fluffy with some yam chunks. It is very quick and easy to make. It makes a perfect dinner or lunch and it is also a party favourite.

List of ingredients

Ingredients Measurement
A tuber of yam 1kg
Red Ball Pepper 3 large balls
Scotch bonnet chillies 1 cup
Onion 2 pieces
Stock cubes 5 cubes
Smoked fish 1 medium sized
Palm oil As desired
Vegetables Optional

Procedure

  • Peel and cut and rinse the yam
  • Blend your pepper mix
  • Cook the yam and the pepper mix together
  • Stir in the Palm oil, sliced onions, and smokedfish
  • Mash the yams
  • Simmer for an additional 3 to 5 minutes.
  • Stir in the herbs or any other leafy vegetable of your choice.
  • Serve and enjoy while still hot!
